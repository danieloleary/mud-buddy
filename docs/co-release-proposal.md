# Co-release Proposal

Mud Buddy should remain independent unless EBMUD explicitly chooses to review, pilot, or co-release it. Until then, the correct public position is:

> Independent prototype for EBMUD customers. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, approved by, or officially reviewed by EBMUD.

## Possible collaboration paths

### 1. Safety review only

EBMUD reviews copy, methodology, official-resource routing, and privacy claims. Dan keeps operating Mud Buddy independently.

### 2. Customer education pilot

EBMUD shares feedback on a customer-safe version focused on high bills, irrigation, usage awareness, and official next steps.

### 3. Conservation partner experiment

Mud Buddy becomes a lightweight companion for conservation education: customers use their own export, get private pattern clues, and are routed to EBMUD conservation and rebate resources.

### 4. Official co-release

Only after written approval, brand review, legal review, accessibility review, privacy review, and operational ownership are defined.

## Copy policy

Do not use these terms unless EBMUD authorizes it in writing:

- official;
- approved;
- endorsed;
- partner;
- partnership;
- co-branded;
- certified;
- EBMUD analysis;
- EBMUD leak detector;
- EBMUD billing tool.

Do not use the EBMUD logo, seal, trade dress, authenticated screenshots, or private portal imagery without permission.

## Minimum review checklist before any shared launch

- Non-affiliation or approved-affiliation language is correct.
- Browser-local privacy claim is reviewed.
- No credential automation exists.
- No account, billing, outage, pressure, water-quality, emergency, rebate, or assistance action is handled by Mud Buddy.
- Report language stays heuristic and non-diagnostic.
- Accessibility and mobile flows are acceptable.
- Support and feedback ownership are clear.
