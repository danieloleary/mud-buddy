# Code of Conduct

Mud Buddy is a small civic-tech project for helping people understand household water data safely. Be kind, practical, and privacy-preserving.

## Expected behavior

- Assume good intent.
- Keep feedback specific and respectful.
- Protect private water-usage data.
- Avoid sharing real EBMUD identifiers, raw rows, authenticated screenshots, or sensitive household context.
- Keep claims modest: possible leak clues and patterns worth checking, not official diagnosis.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory comments.
- Pressuring people to share credentials, account data, or private household details.
- Publishing private usage data or authenticated portal screenshots.
- Representing Mud Buddy as affiliated with or endorsed by EBMUD.

## Reporting

Open a GitHub privacy/safety issue when the concern can be described without private data. If the concern involves private data, describe the category of issue without including the private content.
